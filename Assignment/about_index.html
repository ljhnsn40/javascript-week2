About Us

At Windswept Strands, we're not just a salon; we're a community hub where beauty, culture, and self-expression converge. Specializing in natural hairstyles such as twists, braids, and wig services, our salon was born out of a desire to redefine beauty standards and celebrate the diversity of hair textures and styles. Our journey began with a vision to create a space where clients could not only transform their looks but also embark on a journey of self-discovery and empowerment.

Since our inception, Windswept Strands has been dedicated to pushing the boundaries and elevating the standards of beauty in Memphis and beyond. Our team of skilled stylists and professionals is committed to providing top-notch service and personalized care to every client who walks through our doors. From trendy styles to classic looks, we strive to create an environment where everyone feels welcome, valued, and beautiful. Come join us at Windswept Strands, where your hair dreams become a reality, and your journey to self-confidence begins.
